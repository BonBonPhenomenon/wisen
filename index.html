What is Wisen

Rigorous research,
presented with care

Marketing pricing in New Zealand and Australia is opaque by design. Agencies rarely publish rates. Benchmarks default to US figures. Small businesses end up guessing — or overpaying. Wisen exists to fix that. We research real agency pricing, published benchmarks, and platform data, then present it in a format decision-makers can actually use.

Considered
Every data point is sourced, dated, and scoped to the NZ and AU market.
Transparent
We show our sources and acknowledge where data is estimated or limited.
Direct
Ranges, not vague approximations. Numbers you can use in a conversation.

How we work
01
We research publicly available data
Agency pricing pages, published benchmarks, industry reports, and platform documentation — sourced directly, not aggregated from other summaries.
02
We scope it to NZ and AU
Most benchmarks default to US data. We prioritise local sources, and where NZ-specific data is thin, we apply known market factors and say so clearly.
03
We present ranges, not false precision
Pricing varies. We show realistic ranges by provider type and tier, so you can find where you sit — not just a single number that may not apply to you.
04
We date and source everything
Every report states when research was conducted and which sources it draws on. Markets change — so does our data. Each report is versioned accordingly.
