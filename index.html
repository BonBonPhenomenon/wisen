What is Wisen

Independent research.
Local figures.

Marketing pricing in NZ and AU is opaque by design. Agencies don't publish rates. Freelancers price in a vacuum. Business owners evaluate quotes with no reference point -- and end up overpaying, underspending, or making decisions based on a single number with no context. Wisen researches real agency, contractor, and platform pricing from NZ and AU sources and publishes it here. No paywalls, no US benchmarks relabelled, no affiliation with any provider in the data.

Wisen is operated by Bonnie Vujcich -- marketing practitioner, 20 years across NZ and AU. She built this because she needed it too.

Considered
Every figure is sourced, dated, and scoped to this market. Not borrowed from somewhere else and adjusted.
Transparent
Sources are named. Where NZ data is thin and estimation was required, that is stated clearly.
Direct
Real ranges, by provider type and tier. Numbers you can take into a conversation.

How Wisen works
01
Research from live NZ and AU sources
Agency pricing pages, published benchmarks, industry reports, platform documentation. Sourced directly -- not aggregated from third-party summaries, and not borrowed from overseas markets.
02
Reviewed by Bonnie Vujcich before publication
With 20 years inside NZ and AU marketing -- in-house, contracting, and agency-side -- Bonnie applies direct market knowledge to every figure before it goes live. Where NZ-specific data is thin, that is stated explicitly in the methodology.
03
Ranges, not false precision
Pricing varies by scope, provider, and client. Wisen shows realistic ranges by tier so you can find where a quote sits -- not a single number that may not apply to your situation.
04
Dated, sourced, versioned
Every report states when research was conducted and which sources it draws on. Markets change. Each report carries a version number and research date so you know exactly what you are looking at.
